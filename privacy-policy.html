Privacy Policy

## Our Privacy Policy – Plain &amp; Simple

This policy applies to anyone who uses our services, no matter where they are. It covers all the ways we collect data – online, offline, and through other companies we work with.

It doesn't cover stuff that other websites, services, or apps collect if we don't control them.

### What Info We Grab

We get two types of info: personal (things that identify you) and non-personal (stuff that doesn't).

**Info You Give Us:**

*   Your contact info: Name, email, phone number, and address.
*   Account stuff: Username, password, what you like, and your profile.
*   Payment details: Credit card info, billing address, and what you've bought.
*   What you tell us: Messages, support questions, and survey answers.
*   Job stuff: If you apply for a job with us.

**Info We Snag Automatically:**

*   Device and browser info: IP address, operating system, device ID, browser type.
*   How you use our stuff: What you click on, how long you stay, where you came from.
*   Location: Roughly where you are (if you let us).
*   Cookies and trackers: Bits of code that help us remember you and see how you're using our site.

**Info From Other People:**

*   Login helpers: Like Google, Apple, or Meta.
*   Marketing buddies.
*   Public records and partners.

### What We Do With Your Info

We might use your data to:

*   Keep our services running: Process payments, send you what you order, and help you out.
*   Manage your account: Make sure it's you logging in, remember what you like, and make things personal.
*   Talk to you: Send updates and marketing stuff (if you're cool with it).
*   Make things better: See how people use our stuff and fix what's broken.
*   Keep things safe: Stop fraud and illegal stuff.
*   Follow the rules: Do what the law says we have to.
*   Do research: Make new stuff and figure out what people want.

We'll only use your personal info if we have a good legal reason to.

### Why We Use Your Info (The Legal Stuff)

Depending on where you live, we might use your info because:

*   You said it was okay.
*   We need it to keep our promises to you.
*   The law says we have to.
*   It's good for our business, and it doesn't mess with your rights too much.

### Who We Share With

We don't sell your personal info. But we might share it with:

*   Helpers: Companies that host our site, process payments, keep us secure, etc.
*   Our sister companies: For boring admin stuff.
*   If we get bought: If we merge with another company.
*   The law: If we have to share to follow the law or protect someone.
*   If you say it's okay: If you tell us to share with someone.

Everyone we share with has to keep your data secret and safe.

### Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar stuff to:

*   Remember who you are.
*   Remember what you like.
*   See how people use our site.
*   Show you ads (if you let us).

You can turn off cookies in your browser settings.

### How Long We Keep Your Stuff

We only keep your personal info as long as we need it for the reasons in this policy, unless the law says we have to keep it longer.

When we don't need it anymore, we delete it or scramble it up so nobody knows who it is.

### How We Keep Your Data Safe

We do our best to protect your data with:

*   Encryption (scrambling)
*   Secure data centers
*   Locked-down access
*   Regular security checks

But the internet isn't perfect. You know the risks.

### Where Your Data Goes

Your data might go to other countries, including the United States.

We make sure we follow the rules when we move your data, using things like:

*   Standard legal contracts
*   Checking how safe other countries are
*   Following what the data protection people say

### Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you can:

*   See your personal info
*   Fix or delete it
*   Tell us to stop using it
*   Take back your permission
*   Move your data somewhere else
*   Complain to the authorities

### California Privacy

If you live in California, you have the right to:

*   Know what info we collect about you.
*   Ask us to delete your info.
*   Tell us not to sell or share your data.
*   Not be treated unfairly for using your privacy rights.

### Kids' Privacy

We don't knowingly collect data from kids under 13. If we find out we have it, we'll delete it ASAP.

### Links to Other Sites

We might link to other websites. We're not responsible for their privacy practices. Check their policies before you use them.

### Changes to This Policy

We might update this policy from time to time. We'll post the new version on this page with a new Last Updated date.

If we make big changes, we might email you or show you a notice in our app.
